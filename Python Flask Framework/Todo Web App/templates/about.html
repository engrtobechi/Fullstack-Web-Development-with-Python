About

Welcome to the Todo Web App, a simple and easy-to-use tool for managing your tasks and staying organized.

With our app, you can create new tasks, mark them as complete, and update them as needed. Our intuitive interface makes it easy to keep track of your progress and stay on top of your to-do list.

We are committed to providing a reliable and user-friendly experience for all of our users. If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're always here to help.
